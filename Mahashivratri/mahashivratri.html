About Lord Shiva (Mahadev)

Lord Shiva, also known as Mahadev, Bholenath, and Neelkanth, is one of the principal deities of Hinduism and the supreme yogi of the universe. He represents the destruction of ignorance, ego, and negativity, making way for transformation, renewal, and spiritual awakening.

Seated in deep meditation on Mount Kailash, Lord Shiva symbolizes perfect balance between stillness and cosmic energy. The crescent moon on his head represents time, the river Ganga flowing from his hair signifies purity, and the serpent around his neck shows mastery over fear and death.
