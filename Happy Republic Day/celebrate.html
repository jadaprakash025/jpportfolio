About Republic Day

Republic Day is celebrated on 26th January every year in India to honor the Constitution.

History

India got independence on 15 August 1947, but the Constitution came into force on 26 January 1950.

Indian Constitution

The Constitution of India is the longest written constitution in the world. Dr. B. R. Ambedkar is its architect.

Freedom Fighters

Mahatma Gandhi

Leader of India's freedom movement and pioneer of non-violence.

Bhagat Singh

Brave revolutionary who sacrificed his life for the nation.

Subhas Chandra Bose

Founder of Azad Hind Fauj and a great nationalist leader.

Sardar Patel

Iron Man of India who united the nation.
